The objective is to reach the end of the cave. The map shows all the paths.
The stars (*) denote the movable path.
The dashes (-) denote walls.
The (S) is the starting position.
The (@) denotes your current position.
The (F) denotes finishing line

Move commands:
Type north,south,east or west to move accordingly. Type show map to show the map again

Type "help" for instructions
